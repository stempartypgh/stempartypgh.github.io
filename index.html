Engineering Challenges

From cardboard and duct tape to marble driven computers, STEMparty can revolutionize the party experience.

Based on data-driven methods, STEMparty can bring a rigorous and fun engineering experience to your home or party venue. The basics of engineering take little more than household items, carboard, and vision.

Working with engineering students and educators, we have devised a problem-solution based curriculum that challenges girls and boys to think, innovate, and grow.

Computer Science Builds

Building computers from scratch is cheaper and easier than your think!

With the advent of microcontrollers like the Raspberry Pi and the Hummingbird, building computers that can design games, create Minecraft worlds, and access the internet is easier than ever. We help kids create the computers that will power their innovation, gaming, and learning for years to come!

3D Printing and Design

The future is here, but it can also be at your party.

3D deposition modeling is an emergent technology that will revolutionize supply chains and materials science. With the advent of 3D printing for in-home use, STEMparty can bring 3D design challenges to your party or event.
